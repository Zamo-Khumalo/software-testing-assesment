Product Owner
1. What is the vision/ roadmap of the product?
2. What are the client/ business objectives?
3. Looking at the team's backlog, where does the new feature fall interms of prioritiies?
4. Have the user stories been created and estimated?
5. Looking at the time frames and the resources of the team, how is the capacity of the team? will the team be able to start working oon the new feature?
6. What is the acceptance criteria of the new feature?

UI Designer
1. Has the product research been done and customer needs understood?
2. what are the expected timelines for the creation of personas and information architecture?
3. what are the expected timelines for the completion of the flow design and wireframing?
4. when will the prototype of the feature be available?
5. When is the testing team expected to start with the testing?


Technical Architect
1. What are system requirements for the new feature?
2. Does the new feature change the current architecture diagram?
3. Is there any change required on the current technologies being used?
4. Are there any risks that the new feature may bring?
5. When is the estimated completion time of the architectural diagram?
6. How frquent are code reviews going to be done?
7. What are the expected quality standards and procudures?
